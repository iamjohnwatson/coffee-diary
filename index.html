Coffee Diary Map Guide

Welcome to Akash's Coffee Diary Map! Here's how to use it:

  • Explore Coffee Points: Each bean icon represents a coffee estate or origin.
  • View Details: Click on any bean to see information about that coffee.
  • Change View: Use the globe button in the bottom right to toggle between world view and regional view.
  • Navigation: Zoom in/out with the +/- buttons or mouse wheel. Click and drag to move around the map.

Happy coffee exploring!
